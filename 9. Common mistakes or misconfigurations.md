### Common mistakes or misconfigurations
- The response of Generate Access Token API has a field; “expires_in”: 7200. There could be a misunderstanding that 7200 means 72 hours.
- The criticality and high risk fields should be filled very carefully. As this helps administrators prioritize which access records need stricter monitoring or approval workflows. Also, high risk can lead to additional checks of a profile.
- Forgetting to reload the Access Grid in the UI after calling an API to update data will lead to incorrect data display in the UI.
